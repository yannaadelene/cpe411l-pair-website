Dog Breed Directory

Explore detailed information about the world's most popular dog breeds.

Golden Retriever

Weight 55 - 75 lbs
Life Span 10 - 12 Years
Temperament Devoted, Friendly
Activity Very Active

Golden Retrievers are serious workers at hunting and field work, as guides for the blind, and in search-and-rescue.

German Shepherd

Weight 50 - 90 lbs
Life Span 7 - 10 Years
Temperament Brave, Intelligent
Activity High Energy

German Shepherd Dogs are loyal and protective, often used in police and military roles.
