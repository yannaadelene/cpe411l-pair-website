Cat Breed Directory

Explore detailed information about common cat breeds.

Siamese

Weight 8 - 15 lbs
Life Span 12 - 20 Years
Temperament Vocal, Affectionate
Coat Short, Silky

Siamese cats are among the most social and talkative cats. They thrive on human companionship.

Maine Coon

Weight 10 - 25 lbs
Life Span 12 - 15 Years
Temperament Gentle, Friendly
Coat Long, Shaggy

Known as the "gentle giant," the Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated cat breeds.
