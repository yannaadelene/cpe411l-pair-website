Cat Breed Directory

Explore detailed information about common cat breeds and their unique personalities.

Siamese

Weight 8 - 15 lbs
Life Span 12 - 20 Years
Temperament Vocal, Affectionate
Coat Short, Silky

Siamese cats are among the most social and talkative cats. They thrive on human companionship and are known for their striking blue eyes and point-colored coats.

Maine Coon

Weight 10 - 25 lbs
Life Span 12 - 15 Years
Temperament Gentle, Friendly
Coat Long, Shaggy

Known as the "gentle giant," the Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated cat breeds. They have a sturdy build and are famous for their bushy tails and tufted ears.

Persian

Weight 7 - 12 lbs
Life Span 10 - 15 Years
Temperament Quiet, Sweet
Coat Long, Luxurious

Persians are famous for their round faces and long, flowing coats. They are quiet, docile cats that prefer calm environments and frequent grooming sessions.

Bengal

Weight 8 - 15 lbs
Life Span 12 - 16 Years
Temperament Active, Energetic
Coat Spotted, Marbled

Bengals are highly active and intelligent cats that look like wild leopards. They are very curious, love to climb, and are even known to enjoy playing in water.

Ragdoll

Weight 10 - 20 lbs
Life Span 12 - 17 Years
Temperament Placid, Docile
Coat Semi-long, Soft

Ragdolls get their name because they often go limp with relaxation when held. They are exceptionally calm and affectionate, often following their owners from room to room.

British Shorthair

Weight 9 - 18 lbs
Life Span 12 - 20 Years
Temperament Easygoing, Loyal
Coat Short, Dense

With their round faces and plush coats, British Shorthairs are a beloved breed. They are dignified and quiet, making them excellent companions for those who prefer a low-energy pet.
